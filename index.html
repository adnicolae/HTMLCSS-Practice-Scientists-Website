How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same planet touch, they merge into the next planet of our Solar System!

Note: This is not the official version of 2048. It is based on 2048 (Here). You can play the Cosmos version on your phone via this link. This version was created by Nicolae Andrei for learning and fun purposes only. Each new generated tile represents the next planet of our Solar System (acording to the proximity to Sun), the first tile being the Sun itself.
